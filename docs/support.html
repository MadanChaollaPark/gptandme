GPTandME Support

Support information for the GPTandME Chrome extension.

Before Reporting an Issue

  • Confirm you are using version 1.3.0 or newer for per-service counts.
  • Confirm you are using Chrome/Chromium 111 or newer.
  • Confirm the current site is one of the supported hosts: ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or Perplexity.
  • Reload the extension after updating it.
  • Send a new prompt after the extension is loaded; historical prompts are not backfilled.
  • Usage recorded before service attribution was introduced may appear under the provider "unknown" rather than being assigned to a service.
  • Reset today also clears session aggregates and recent deduplication IDs, while preserving older daily, service, and model totals.

What To Include

For help with GPTandME, include your browser version, extension version, supported website, whether the combined or per-service count is affected, and whether you sent the prompt with the keyboard or the send button. Do not include prompt text or conversation URLs.

CSV Compatibility

Version 1.3.0 exports date,provider,model,count. Legacy GPTandME CSV files that use date,model,count remain importable and are recorded with provider unknown.

Privacy

Review the GPTandME privacy policy for details about local storage, supported hosts, and data handling.
